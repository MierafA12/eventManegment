About EventZone

EventZone is a simple, user-friendly platform for browsing tech events, registering online, and reserving your seat — all without any backend setup! Perfect for learners and small organizations managing events.

What You Can Do

Explore Events

Browse a list of upcoming tech-related events with date, time, and venue information. Stay updated and never miss an opportunity.

Register & Login

Create an account and log in using your email and password. Your data is saved safely in your browser using local storage.

Book an Event

Reserve a seat in an available event. The booking is saved to your account and you can review your reservations anytime.

Fake Payment

Simulate a payment process to confirm your event booking — no real money required, just a simple confirmation screen!

View Booked Events

After login, you can access your profile page and see a list of all events you've reserved, directly from local storage.

Contact & Feedback

Get in touch through the contact page and share your feedback. We're here to help you with any questions.
